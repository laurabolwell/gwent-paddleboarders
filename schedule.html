Tuesday

Llandegfedd Lake

On the water for 5pm

We launch from directly in front of the watersports centre.
what3words: ///example.leotard.rags
Parking: £3 charge payable at machine (accepts card only)
There is a £10 charge to launch at the reservoir, payable directly to Llandegfedd Lake (memberships available). This includes the use of lockers, changing rooms and showers.
Please bring a drink and snack with you onto the lake and ensure you are dressed apppropriately for the weather and conditions.

Safety Information

This is a social paddle. All participants are responsible for their own safety and must paddle within their own competency levels.

A PFD and a leash (ankle or waist) are mandatory to join the group.

Thursday

Cardiff Bay

On the water for 5:30pm

We launch from Channel View Leisure Centre.
what3words: ///next.raves.plank
Parking: included with launch permit
There is a £5 charge payable at reception. This includes a launch permit, parking, and the use of showers and changing rooms.
Please bring a drink and snack with and ensure you are dressed apppropriately for the weather and conditions.

Safety Information

This is a social paddle. All participants are responsible for their own safety and must paddle within their own competency levels.

A PFD and quick release waistbelt are mandatory to join the group.
