Who are we?

We are community of paddle boarders with a sense of fun and adventure, connecting people across the Gwent and South Wales area who want to explore the local waterways with like-minded individuals.
It is free to join our paddles, however you will need to pay a launch fee at some locations.
No need to book, just turn up and join us!

Please note we do not offer instruction or equipment hire. Paddlers must have completed a minimum of an introduction to SUP course before joining us.
